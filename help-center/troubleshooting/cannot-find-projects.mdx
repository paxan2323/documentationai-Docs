---
title: Cannot Find My Projects
description: How to solve missing projects in your dashboard.
---

## Problem

You log in but do not see your projects listed under your Andrew Piven dashboard.

## Possible causes
- Projects not yet created
- Account mismatch (wrong email)
- Browser cache issues

## Solution
<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify login email">
    Double-check you are logged in with the correct email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh browser cache">
    Hard-refresh the dashboard page (Ctrl+Shift+R or Cmd+Shift+R).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a new project">
    If this is your first visit, click "New Project" to get started.
  </Step>
</Steps>
