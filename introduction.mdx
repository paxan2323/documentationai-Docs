## Overview

Andrew Piven provides a centralized space to organize, manage, and collaborate on your project documentation. You create structured docs, version content, and share knowledge efficiently without scattered files or tools. Start building your knowledge base today.

<Callout kind="info">
Andrew Piven supports Markdown, MDX components, and custom workflows for teams of all sizes.
</Callout>

## Benefits for Project Teams

You streamline workflows by centralizing all project docs in one place. Teams reduce onboarding time with searchable guides and interactive examples. Maintain consistency across repositories, and scale documentation as your projects grow. Developers, PMs, and designers collaborate seamlessly without email chains or wiki sprawl.

## Quick Start

Get up and running in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Account" icon="user-plus">
    Sign up at `https://app.andrewpiven.com/register` with your email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="New Workspace" icon="plus">
    Click "New Workspace" and name it for your project, like "MyProject Docs".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add First Page" icon="file-plus">
    Select "Introduction" template to bootstrap your docs.

````markdown
```markdown
# Welcome to MyProject

This is your starting point.
```
````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish" icon="globe">
    Hit publish to generate your live site at `https://docs.andrewpiven.com/myproject`.
  </Step>
</Steps>
